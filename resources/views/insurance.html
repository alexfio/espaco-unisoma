Payment & Insurance

We are committed to providing you with the best therapy for your needs, and making sure that payment and insurance are easy. We believe that, as our client, you deserve to:

Keep Your Information Private

Insurance companies may require that you share extensive personal information before covering your treatment. We don’t.

read more

Have Therapy Goals Tailored to You

Insurance companies often dictate things like how long you can stay in therapy. We let you decide.

read more

Use Your Existing Insurance

Many practices don’t help with submitting out-of-network paperwork or helping you get reimbursed. We do.

read more

Use Familiar Payment Methods

Many practices and insurance companies make it difficult to manage your payment. We make it easy.

read more

We’re out-of-network so that we can collaborate with you

Your therapy

won’t be controlled by bureaucrats.

We Keep Your Sessions Private

In-network providers are required to divulge your therapy history and other personal information to insurance companies and their business partners. We stay independent.

We Decide on Your Therapy Goals With You

By directly working with you, a third-party insurance company — who knows next to nothing about you — can’t dictate your therapy.

You Can Almost Always Use Your Insurance

Our fees are almost always partially reimbursed through your out-of-network insurance coverage benefit.

It varies, but most of our clients are reimbursed 50-80% of our fees after they have met their annual deductible.

Payment & Reimbursement Made Easier

We can file your reimbursement request for you, making the process much less of a hassle.

We accept all major credit cards, checks, and cash, and to keep it simple we ask that you pay at the time that you meet with us.

We’re accepting new patients

Talk to us today - we often get back to you in under an hour.
